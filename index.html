Make Everything Easier with the myMTN NG App

The myMTN NG app simplifies your mobile life. This app gives you complete access to easily manage your account.

With the myMTN NG app, you can enjoy exciting data offers, your data usage, airtime, share data, buy and stay up to date with lifestyle content.

You can also check your SIM profile details, activate data bundles, check your data and airtime balance, and link your NIN

to your line with the myMTN NG app. The app is FREE to use and is available for all users to download on Google Playstore and Apple Appstore.
